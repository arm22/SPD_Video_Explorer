SPD Body Camera Map

{% for child in maphtml["body"] %} {{child|safe}} {% endfor %}

The Seattle Police Department has started to release footage of officers now equipped with body cameras. Accessing this data is difficult, and tying it to public crime records; near impossible.

With this tool the public can view released footage from SPD body cams, and gather information about local crime. We hope this puts our city's crime data where it belongs, in the public domain.

*Disclaimer: SPD video footage is redacted. Some footage may still be found offensive. Please report any knowledge of a crime to your local authorities.*
